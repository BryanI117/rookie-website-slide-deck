Final remarks

StarryNight has gone down as a classic example of net art, to take advantage of the internet and make something that can't be done anywhere else, in this case, make an exciting take on forum archives. Not only that, it did its job of archiving the vital exchanges between late 90s net artists, even if some of it's lost. Since its creation in 1999, this art piece has been exhibited throughout the world, from Austria to Spain.
